Short Story

We aim to provide our customers with a local whole grain bread experience that allows them to feel connected to our community, family and the products that they are eating. Our business takes pride in using natural, local ingredients whenever possible and forming connections with local small businesses and farms who share the same appreciation for good food and great bread.

We can promise that you will prefer to enjoy our fresh baked artisan breads and pastries over the processed products that you can find at the grocery store.
